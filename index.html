About

I am a Postdoctoral Research Scholar in the Department of Psychology at the University of California, Riverside. I received my Ph.D. in Personality Psychology from the University of California, Davis (2025), where I worked with Richard W. Robins. My research draws on longitudinal data to examine how personality traits, temperament, and socio-contextual factors shape individuals' development across childhood, adolescence, and early adulthood — with a particular focus on educational outcomes and motivation among Mexican-origin youth.
